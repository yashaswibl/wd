Your CCTV security system is only as good as the hard disc backing it. A CCTV camera is just the beginning of a surveillance system, the seeing eye. WD PurpleTM hard drives are the heart and brain of surveillance, powering the entire dynamic to keep you safe, without skipping a beat.

The whole purpose of installing CCTV cameras is to be able to clearly record what is happening and to go back and check the footage whenever needed. General hard-drives are meant to handle extremely lighter work-loads compared to surveillance HDDs and are likely to fail easily from overuse. The storage device for a security camera system must be able to record, store and re-play videos non-stop from multiple feeds. In case of any technical glitch that can cause hang-ups, the hard drive must move on to capture the next frame, or risk losing crucial footage.

WD PurpleTM hard drives are designed specifically to handle heavy workloads from CCTV security cameras and come with spy-like intelligence, knowing what details to focus on.

WD PurpleTM microSD Cards for
Edge Surviellance & Back-Up
You have probably used microSD cards to extend the memory of your smartphone or laptop. These cards can surely store digital files, but storing video captures from 24X7 on cameras is a different ballgame altogether. You would delete your stored video footage when you do not need it and format the card to clear up space for new footage. WD PurpleTM MicroSDTM cards come with an endurance of 500 Program/Erase cycles: you can format your card about 500 times. Retail grade microSD cards come without any endurance specifications and will wear down much faster when used for surveillance. It’s important to know in advance if your microSD card requires maintenance service, especially when it’s a critical part of your security system and placed somewhere hard-to-reach. WD PurpleTM MicroSDTM cards come with a Card Health Monitor that checks on the card health and alerts you in advance when maintenance is required. PurpleTM MicroSDTM cards come with a 3-year warranty, whereas surveillance use is not covered in the warranty for retail grade microSD cards.
